# ✂️ clipforge - Turn long videos into vertical clips

ClipForge turns long YouTube videos into short, vertical clips. It handles transcription, adds subtitles, and crops video content automatically. You keep your files private on your own computer.

## 📋 What this tool does

Many people want to share long videos on social media, but full videos do not fit the vertical format of platforms like TikTok or Instagram. ClipForge solves this problem. It identifies the best parts of your video, crops them to fit a phone screen, and puts text on the screen so viewers can follow along without sound. 

The software runs on your computer. You do not need to upload your videos to a website or wait in cloud queues. You control every step of the process.

## 🚀 Setting up your first clip

After you launch ClipForge, the application opens a window on your desktop. This is your workspace where you manage your files and settings.

### Adding a video

1. Click the "New Job" button inside the app.
2. Paste the link to the YouTube video you want to process.
3. Click the "Fetch Video" button. The software downloads the video file to your machine. 
4. Wait for the progress bar to show that the download is complete.

### Configuring your output

The tool offers several ways to customize how your video looks:

* **Crop style:** Choose if you want the app to stay in the center or track people as they move.
* **Subtitles:** The app creates subtitles automatically. You can turn these on or off here.
* **Format:** Select the aspect ratio that fits your social media goals. Most users choose 9:16 for vertical viewing.

### Processing the file

Once your settings are ready, click the "Start Clip" button. The software now performs three main steps:

1. **Transcription:** It listens to the audio and converts the speech into text.
2. **Analysis:** It scans the video to find the most interesting moments.
3. **Encoding:** It cuts the video, burns the subtitles into the image, and saves the final file.

This process takes time based on the length of your video and the speed of your computer processor. You can monitor the progress on the main dashboard.

## ❓ Troubleshooting common issues

If the software does not work as expected, check these common fixes:

* **Slow performance:** High-resolution videos take more power to process. Close other programs while you create clips to give ClipForge more resources.
* **Missing subtitles:** Ensure your video has clear audio. If the speaker mumbles or there is loud background noise, the software may struggle to create the text.
* **Video errors:** Some YouTube videos include age restrictions or privacy settings. The tool cannot download videos that are not available to the public. 
* **Update the app:** Check the release page often. New versions contain improvements for better transcription and faster encoding speeds.

## 🔒 Your data and privacy

ClipForge performs every action locally. This means your private videos never leave your machine. The software does not send your data to any servers, and it does not store your history on a website. You own your output. 

Everything stays in the folder where you install the app. If you delete a project, the software erases the source file and the finished clip from your hard drive immediately.
